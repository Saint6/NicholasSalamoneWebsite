Flamel

A side scrolling action-adventure platforme, where you play as the alchemist Nicolas Flamel on his journey to find the fabled philosphers stone. The game was created using the unreal engine using assets purchased from the Unreal store. I worked on the programming and the game/level design for the levels. The project was for my capston project to earn my undergraduate degree from Ferris State University.
